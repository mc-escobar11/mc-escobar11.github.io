About me

Maria C. Escobar

I am a PhD candidate in Engineering and a researcher at the Center for Research and Formation in Artificial Intelligence (CINFONIA) supervised by Prof. Pablo Arbeláez. I previously did a Research Internship at Google, working on pose estimation methods for Project Starline. My main interests include Computer Vision and Deep Learning, as well as their role in human understanding through embodied AI. When I’m not running experiments, I am usually crocheting, dancing or volunteering.
